      How our AI document check works

      Every document is reviewed twice

      When you upload a file, it's first read by an AI model trained to check documents against embassy requirements — passport validity windows, photo specifications, blank-page counts, expiry dates, and consistency across your file. This happens in under a minute. Anything the AI is confident about is marked passed; anything it's unsure of, or that fails a rule, is flagged for a human visa specialist before your application is ever filed.
